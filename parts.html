    Parts Revenue Programme

    Our Parts Revenue Program empowers Airlines, Leasing Companies, and MROs by maximizing the value of misdiagnosed and neglected engine parts. From the moment you engage with us, our dedicated team seamlessly takes charge of the entire process, ensuring a smooth transfer of consigned parts to our warehouse.

    Why PRP?

    Innovative Pre-inspection Process:

    Our innovative pre-inspection process is the cornerstone of our established track record. This crucial step guarantees that only parts with the potential for overhaul reach the best repair shops, ensuring maximum value for our consignors.

    Extensive Network of Buyers:

    With an extensive network of buyers accepting both OEM and FAA/EASA approved repairs, our meticulous process further enhances the value proposition for our consignors.

    Flexible Options:

    Our Parts Revenue Program offers flexible options tailored to your specific needs. Choose between consignment or one-time purchase offers, providing the flexibility you require.

    Transparency and Reporting:

    Transparency is paramount to us. Our monthly reports provide consignors with complete visibility, while regular status updates and performance reporting keep you informed throughout the parts management journey.

    Faster Turn-around Times (TAT):

    We understand the importance of time. Consignors can expect speedy processing and swift distribution of funds, ensuring faster turnaround times (TAT) from consignment to fund distribution.
