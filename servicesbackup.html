Parts Sales

At Target Aero Engines, we take pride in presenting a comprehensive selection of overhauled parts to our esteemed network of buyers, including OEMs, end users, and brokers. Our meticulously sourced parts originate from new-generation engines CFM6-5B/7B, V2500A5, CF680C2, PW4000, and GE90 crafted by industry leaders such as CFM International, GE, IAE, and Pratt & Whitney.

Transparency is one of our core values, reflecting our commitment to only quote what we have in inventory.

Discover our extensive inventory available for sale on the dedicated “Parts for Sale” page of this website. Here, users can effortlessly search for parts based on engine type and part number, streamlining the process of finding the perfect component for your needs. Additionally, our parts are also listed on ILSmart.com, b2b-aero, StockMarket.aero.
