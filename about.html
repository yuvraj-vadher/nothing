    Our Story

    We take pride in being a specialty aviation service company dedicated to serving the global airline industry with unwavering integrity. Based in Las Vegas, Nevada, Target Aero Engines holds the prestigious ASA-100 certification, reflecting our commitment to the highest standards of quality and excellence.

    Our management team, boasting over 50 years of collective aviation experience, recognizes that true success is rooted in a superior client experience and a consistent, reliable, and honest approach to business. Since our establishment in 2022, Target Aero Engines has remained steadfast in upholding these principles.

    When you choose Target Aero Engines as your aviation service provider, you embark on a journey marked by unparalleled quality and transparency. We understand the significance of cost management and have cultivated a robust network of valued business partners, fostering mutually beneficial relationships.

    Explore the possibilities of a seamless aviation experience with Target Aero Engines. Whether you are an airline, an MRO, a Leasing Company, or a broker, our commitment to excellence is designed to exceed your expectations.

    For inquiries or to discover how Target Aero Engines can be of assistance to you, please visit the ‘Contact Us’ page on this website. We look forward to the opportunity to serve you.
