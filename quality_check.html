    Quality Check

    At Target Aero Engines, our dedication to excellence is epitomized by our unwavering commitment to the ASA-100 Quality System accreditation in compliance with FAA Advisory Circular 00-56B. This commitment is the cornerstone of our mission to provide top-tier service and ensure 100% customer satisfaction.

    Principles

    Integrity and Transparency

    We prioritize openness and honesty in our dealings with customers, maintaining a high level of integrity in every job we undertake.

    Employee Success and Satisfaction

    We Foster an environment that promotes the success and job satisfaction of our employees. Recognizing that motivated and content employees contribute to the overall success of our organization, we prioritize their well- being.

    Employee Training and Adherence

    All our employees undergo effective training to ensure a deep understanding and strict adherence to our Quality System requirements, contributing to the consistent delivery of high-quality services.

    Communication Excellence

    We commit to maintaining open lines of communication and responding promptly to each inquiry or request, ensuring a seamless and efficient customer experience.

    Continuous Improvement

    We actively monitor and measure key outputs of our Quality System, with a relentless pursuit of continuous improvement. This ensures that our processes evolve to meet the ever-changing needs and expectations of our customers.

    This Quality Policy Statement serves as an unequivocal expression of our commitment to the ASA-100 Quality System. It is not merely a choice but an imperative in achieving our organizational goals, and it will be upheld at every level of our organization.
